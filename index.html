About Me

I have a small garden that I tend every morning; it is the best time to start a day for me. Gardening is my meditation.

Seldom I will get bored; I travel to places through the mind. People think I am good-tempered, in actuality, my mind finds things to do. It includes that I engage myself in problem-solving, which I enjoy very much. Through every trial-and-error, I learn something. I appreciate that mistakes, uncomfortable or not, are windows of opportunity for me to become stronger and better.

The world is big and beautiful that I have not traveled much. I hope to do it someday with my sketchbook. Would you think the Bootcamp will bring me closer to the dream?
