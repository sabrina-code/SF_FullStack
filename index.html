About Me

I always hope to be a developer who designs or a designer who codes. Technology is advanced in such a way that more creative freedom could be enjoyed if one is versatile and good at the tools.

Gardening is my meditation every day. I have a small garden that I tend in the morning; it is the best way for me to start a day.

In the morning, the first few words I speak make me happy.

"Good morning, Rudy. Time for a walkie."

Rudy is my dog.
